About

My name is Jonathan Andrade Valenzuela and I am currently in my 3rd year of university studying Political Science. I have aspirations to work in the tech industry as a fullstack software engineer. Many people tend to ask about why I am studying Political Science when I want to be a SWE, and my answer to them is always quite long & personal, but to sum it up, I have grown up with a passion for political science, however as time has passed on, I could not see myself working in the industry. I took some time to reevaluate my plan, and I came to the conclusion that I can see myself working in tech as a career. I have a passion for creating things, and programming is the perfect outlet for me to do so.
